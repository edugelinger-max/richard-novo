WHERE to buy

At Fiszman, true luxury is permanence and meaning. Each piece is born from the earth, shaped by history, refined by culture and design with craftsmanship and artistry.

Fiszman pieces have been exhibited at several Couture Shows, at retail locations including Bergdorf Goodman in New York City and are currently available at Marissa Collections in Palm Beach and The Jewelry Edit.

MEDIA COVERAGE

The quality and the provenance of the gems used in Fiszman pieces have not only captured the attention of consumers, but also experts from gem and gemology magazines abroad who have documented the importance of his designs and craftsmanship.
